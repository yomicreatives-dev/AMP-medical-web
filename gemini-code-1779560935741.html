Who We Are

Dedicated to Your Complete Rehabilitation

At Athenkosi Mbetshu Physiotherapists Inc., we balance evidence-based manual therapy, structural physical therapy modalities, and target-driven functional rehabilitation frameworks to address individual lifestyle conditions.

Our comprehensive protocols assist you throughout your physical recovery—whether you are aiming to heal from intense athletic injuries, managing continuous neuro-musculoskeletal pain profiles, or navigating delicate post-operative recovery timelines.

Clinical Scope

Conditions We Treat

Spinal Health & Back Pain

Comprehensive therapy for acute lower back pain, neck stiffness, slipped discs, sciatica, and structural posture corrections.

Sports Injuries & Recovery

Targeted diagnostics and tissue healing for muscle tears, ligament sprains, tendonitis, and joint instabilities.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Structured protocols following total knee/hip replacements, ligament reconstructions, and delicate orthopedic surgeries.

Neurological Conditions

Rehabilitative exercises to optimize mobility, motor control, balance, and independence following a stroke or related disorders.

Chronic Pain Frameworks

Integrated neuromuscular stimulation and functional pathways for ongoing arthritic and muscular conditions.

Respiratory Physiotherapy

Clinical chest physiotherapy designed to optimize breathing mechanics and clear airway congestion safely.

Specialized Care

Corporate Ergonomics & Assessment

Biomechanical adjustments and custom desk posture configurations designed to minimize desk-bound occupational stress, repetitive strain complications, and persistent postural strain injuries.

Wellness

Geriatric Functional Mobility

Gentle, progressive physical regimens dedicated to preserving independence, strengthening stabilizer muscle groups, and improving motor balance to mitigate slip risks in older adults.
