About the Practice

Athenkosi Mbetshu
Physiotherapists Inc.

We are a physiotherapy practice based in East London, Eastern Cape, with branches in Mdantsane and Oxford Street, Belgravia. Our practice delivers evidence-based physical rehabilitation, injury prevention, and wellness services.

Our goal is to help patients restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve overall physical function — through personalised, goal-directed treatment plans built around each patient's unique needs.

We are proud to be recognised providers for Chronic Back and Neck Rehabilitation (CBNR) and conduct Road Accident Fund (RAF) medicolegal assessments.

Your First Visit

What to Expect

01

Detailed Consultation

Your physiotherapist will discuss your condition in detail — your history, symptoms, and goals. Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to the area being assessed.

02

Physical Assessment

A thorough evaluation of your movement, strength, and function is conducted to understand the full picture of your condition.

03

Personalised Plan

Your findings are explained and a tailored treatment plan is developed around your needs — including hands-on therapy and exercise rehabilitation.

04

Agreed Goals

Treatment goals and session frequency are agreed upon during your first visit, so you leave with clarity and a clear path forward.

Initial assessment: approximately 1 hour
Follow-up sessions: 45 – 60 minutes
